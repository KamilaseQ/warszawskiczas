# Project Constitution — Warszawski Czas

## Mission
Build a premium, prestige-building, lead-generating website for Warszawski Czas first.
The future owner app exists to reduce chaos and improve daily execution, but it must never derail the website-first priority.

## Non-negotiable truths
- The website comes first.
- The website must look exceptional, premium, and coherent.
- Prestige, trust, scanability, and lead conversion must coexist.
- The business is a small luxury boutique, not a mass ecommerce store.
- Lead volume is expected to stay relatively low.
- Simplicity beats enterprise complexity.
- Mokotowska 71 is a strategic brand asset.
- The owner app is for one core operational user.
- The future app must stay practical, fast, and low-friction.
- Never invent business policies or factual claims that are not confirmed.

## Strategic priorities
1. Beautiful and status-building public website
2. Strong forms and contact paths
3. Product offer management that is easy to update
4. Search visibility built through real quality, not tricks
5. Later: simple owner PWA for daily organization

## Operating principles
- Always separate: MVP / Later / Unnecessary
- Always choose the simpler architecture that still supports future growth
- Always challenge ideas that add complexity without clear value
- Always preserve premium feel
- Always preserve maintainability

## Hard prohibitions
- Do not bloat the product with unnecessary features
- Do not turn the site into a generic ecommerce template
- Do not turn the future app into a bloated CRM
- Do not create duplicate sources of truth for offer data
- Do not invent return policy, sourcing timelines, payment terms, verification procedures, or warranty details
