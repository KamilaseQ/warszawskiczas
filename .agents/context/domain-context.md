# Domain Context

## Business type
Warszawski Czas is a luxury boutique in Warsaw focused on expensive watches and jewelry.

## Public website role
The website is:
- a prestige asset
- a lead-generation machine
- a curated product/catalog experience
- a trust and expertise signal

## Sales reality
The business relies on:
- trust
- discretion
- taste
- expertise
- premium service
- returning clients
- selective product communication

## Lead categories
- purchase from offer
- special order / sourcing
- selling a watch
- consignment
- service
- general inquiry

## Customer model
A client is persistent over time.
The same person may:
- buy a watch
- return later for service
- return later for another product
- ask for sourcing
Never model the same person as disconnected duplicate client entities.

## Offer logic
Public product statuses:
- available with price
- available on request
- unavailable with price

## Communication priorities
For MVP, the critical flows are:
- forms
- email
- phone number
- trust-building content
- luxury unique feeling
