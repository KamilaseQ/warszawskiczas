# wc-product-offer-management

## Purpose
Keep offer editing fast and practical while preserving one source of truth.

## Use when
Product model, offer editing, product statuses, price visibility, watch descriptions, internal editing access.

## Project truths
- Owner must later be able to add a watch.
- Owner must be able to change status, price, and watch description.
- Owner should not be editing general website copy in the operational workflow.
- Public statuses:
  - available with price
  - available on request
  - unavailable

## Rules
- Keep product data separate from editorial page content.
- Keep one source of truth.
- Make mobile-oriented operational edits fast.
- Design for later owner app consumption.

## Guardrails
- No duplicate product systems.
- No mixing product operations with full-site content editing.
- No overbuilt admin for a small boutique.

## Output format
- Data shape
- Permissions
- Editing flow
- Sync implications
