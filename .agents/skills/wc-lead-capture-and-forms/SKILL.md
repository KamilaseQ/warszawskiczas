# wc-lead-capture-and-forms

## Purpose
Design contact and lead-capture flows that convert without damaging the premium experience.

## Use when
Forms, CTAs, contact blocks, conversion sections, lead-routing logic.

## Project truths
- Forms are part of MVP.
- Form categories:
  - purchase from offer
  - special order / sourcing
  - selling a watch
  - consignment
  - service
  - general inquiry

## Rules
- Keep forms short unless extra qualification improves quality.
- Match the CTA language to the visitor intent.
- Make category choice clear.
- Reduce friction while preserving premium tone.
- Use trust-building cues before asking for contact.

## Guardrails
- No generic low-context forms where a specific one is better.
- No cheap pushy copy.
- No unnecessary fields.

## Output format
- Goal
- Suggested fields
- CTA copy direction
- Lead category logic
- Why this is premium and effective
