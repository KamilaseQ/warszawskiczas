# wc-brand-and-luxury-experience

## Purpose
Keep all public-facing work aligned with the premium identity of Warszawski Czas.

## Use when
Homepage, sections, copy direction, layout rhythm, visual decisions, brand communication, product presentation.

## Rules
- The site must feel curated, not mass-market.
- Build trust and desire without cheap hype.
- Use restraint, elegance, and clarity.
- Preserve strong visual hierarchy and scanability.
- Treat Mokotowska 71 as a prestige signal.
- Make every contact path feel high-trust.

## Guardrails
- No generic ecommerce language.
- No cluttered layouts.
- No visual gimmicks that weaken sophistication.

## Output format
- Page/section role
- Emotional goal
- Trust signals
- Premium risks
- Recommendation
