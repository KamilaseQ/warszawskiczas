# content-guardrails-wc

## Purpose
Prevent invented or risky business claims from appearing in public or internal content.

## Use when
Copywriting, FAQs, product pages, service pages, trust sections, policy-adjacent text.

## Rules
- If a business fact is not confirmed, do not invent it.
- For sensitive details, route toward direct contact.
- Keep claims defensible and elegant.

## Never invent
- return policy
- sourcing timelines
- payment terms
- verification/authentication procedures
- warranty details
- service conditions not explicitly confirmed

## Output format
- Safe claim set
- Unknowns redirected to contact
- Risk notes
