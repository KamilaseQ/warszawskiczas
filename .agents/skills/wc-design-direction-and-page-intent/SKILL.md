# wc-design-direction-and-page-intent

## Purpose
Define the overall design logic of the Warszawski Czas website and the emotional/business intent of each public page, so the whole website feels coherent but not repetitive.

## Use when
Use for homepage design, page planning, section planning, UI decisions, visual hierarchy, copy-structure alignment, and any task involving public-facing website experience.

## Do not use when
Do not use for backend, admin workflows, internal tools, or purely technical refactors unrelated to user-facing design.

## Project truths
- Warszawski Czas is a luxury boutique, not a mass ecommerce store.
- The website must build prestige, trust, desire, exclusivity, and premium brand perception.
- Mokotowska 71 is a strategic brand asset.
- The website must feel elegant, editorial, selective, and premium.
- The website must support lead generation, but without looking like an aggressive sales page.
- Dark green and gold are supporting accent colors, not dominant visual noise.

## Global design rules
- Use strong editorial hierarchy and generous spacing.
- Create visual rhythm across sections and pages.
- Keep layouts highly scannable.
- Use serif-display feeling in headings and clean readable body typography.
- Use dark green and gold sparingly and tastefully.
- Avoid patterns especialy generic ecommerce patterns, SaaS patterns, cyberpunk aesthetics, clutter, heavy shadows, and cheap luxury clichés.
- Each page must share the same brand language but express a different emotional role.

## Page intent rules

### Homepage /
Business role:
- flagship brand impression
- prestige
- desire
- first trust
- movement deeper into the site

Emotional role:
- strongest “wow”, but still classy

Design direction:
- most representative page
- strongest visual hierarchy
- best section rhythm
- editorial and premium

Avoid:
- making it too catalog-like
- making it too ordinary
- making it too informational
- making it too crowded

### Offer /Oferta
Business role:
- premium catalog
- selective browsing
- product discovery
- lead generation through exploration, not checkout

Emotional role:
- desire
- selective interest
- boutique curation

Design direction:
- more ordered and product-focused than homepage
- premium catalog, not generic ecommerce grid

Avoid:
- marketplace feel
- dense shopping layouts
- discount/storefront aesthetics

### Hidden collection /ukryta-kolekcja
Business role:
- create exclusivity
- create a sense of uniqueness and inaccessibility
- generate curiosity
- drive contact for exceptional pieces

Emotional role:
- discreet exclusivity
- private access
- sophistication without gimmicks

Design direction:
- more misterious
- more restrained
- more negative space
- more suggestion than exposition

Avoid:
- dark cliché luxury
- dramatic secret-club aesthetics

### Services /uslugi
Business role:
- establish competence and trust beyond product sales

Emotional role:
- credibility
- expertise
- assurance

Design direction:
- more structured and informative
- still premium, but less theatrical than homepage

Avoid:
- generic service-company layouts
- overly technical presentation
- dry utility-only design

### Service /uslugi/naprawa-i-serwis
Business role:
- maximize trust and perceived safety

Emotional role:
- calm confidence
- responsibility
- precision

Design direction:
- clean, restrained, reassuring

Avoid:
- visual overperformance
- unnecessary drama

### Purchase /uslugi/skup
Business role:
- reduce distrust
- show fair conditions and professionalism
- encourage discreet inquiry
- show professional process

Emotional role:
- seriousness
- fairness
- control

Design direction:
- structured, stable, transparent-feeling

Avoid:
- aggressive conversion style
- suspicious “quick cash” energy

### Consignment /uslugi/komis
Business role:
- show boutique-led representation as a better alternative to informal selling channels
- show fair conditions and professionalism

Emotional role:
- partnership
- curation
- confidence

Design direction:
- relational, selective, premium

Avoid:
- “we sell anything” vibe
- generic classifieds logic

### Boutique /butik
Business role:
- turn the brand into a real destination
- make Mokotowska 71 emotionally meaningful

Emotional role:
- place-based desire
- atmosphere
- experiential trust

Design direction:
- space, mood, physical presence, boutique experience

Avoid:
- generic about page structure
- bio-heavy storytelling disconnected from place

### Contact /kontakt
Business role:
- remove friction and convert interest into contact or visit

Emotional role:
- calm accessibility
- concierge-style welcome
- confidence without pressure

Design direction:
- simple, elegant, highly legible, premium contact point

Avoid:
- boring utility-only contact page
- aggressive lead-page behavior

## Required questions before designing any page
- What is the business goal of this page?
- What emotional state should it create?
- How should it differ from the homepage while staying in the same brand system?

## Guardrails
- Do not make all pages feel the same.
- Do not apply the homepage visual intensity to every page.
- Do not use accent colors in a loud or decorative way.

## Checklist
- Is the page’s role clear?
- Is the emotional intent clear?
- Is the visual intensity correct for this page?
- Does it fit the same brand system?
- Does it avoid generic patterns from ecommerce, SaaS, or cheap luxury websites?

## Output format
- Page goal
- Emotional goal
- Visual direction
- Section logic
- What to avoid
