# wc-local-trust-signals

## Purpose
Strengthen credibility, relevance, and prestige through local signals.

## Use when
Location section, contact page, trust-building copy, local SEO, map/address logic, brand narrative around place.

## Rules
- Treat Mokotowska 71 as a strategic signal.
- Make location part of the experience narrative.
- Use local trust cues naturally.
- Reinforce that a visit/contact is a premium experience.

## Guardrails
- Do not reduce location to a throwaway footer line.
- Do not overdo “local SEO wording” in a spammy way.

## Output format
- Trust role
- Local signals to surface
- Where to surface them
- Tone notes
